About Manu's

Emmanuel "Manu" Mkhize is a South African businessman and entrepreneur who has spent the last 23 years in the business of shoe manufacturing. Starting out as a worker on the line, Manu's interest in designing and making shoes grew. In 2010 Manu got into a partnership with some of the local brands where he would help design their shoes as well as sell them.


Now Manu has sought the help of the internet as a platform to sell his and his local colleague's shoes which range from safety shoes to leisure shoes, as well as sneakers developed with the highest quality.
